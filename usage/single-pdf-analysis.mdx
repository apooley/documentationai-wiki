title: Single PDF Analysis
description: How to process a single PDF eBook and create a WooCommerce product draft.
---

## Overview

The **Single PDF** tab is designed for processing one eBook at a time. This is the ideal workflow when you want to immediately review the AI's suggestions and create a product draft.

To begin, navigate to **WooCommerce > eBook Assistant** and ensure the **Single PDF** tab is selected.

## Step 1: Upload and Analyze

1. **Select a PDF file**: Click the file input to choose a PDF from your computer. The file must contain selectable text (scanned images without OCR are not supported).
2. **Sample pages (Optional)**: Enter comma-separated page numbers (e.g., `3, 12, 45`) that you want the plugin to extract as gallery images. Page 1 is always extracted automatically as the cover image.
3. **Targeting / context notes (Optional)**: Provide specific instructions to the AI. For example, *"Focus the description on beginners,"* or *"This is part of the Summer Collection."*
4. Click **Generate Product Draft**.

The plugin will extract the text locally, summarize it, and send the summary to your configured AI provider.

<Callout kind="info">
The time required depends on the size of the PDF and the response time of the AI provider. You will see progress indicators as the extraction and analysis stages complete.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Review AI Suggestions

Once analysis is complete, the interface will expand to show several sections detailing the AI's findings:

- **PDF extraction**: Shows how many characters were extracted and if the text was truncated to meet your configured limits.
- **Summary**: A brief overview of the eBook's content generated by the AI.
- **Store pricing**: Statistical data (min, max, average, median) based on the context of your existing WooCommerce products.
- **Similar products**: Existing products in your store that the AI identified as similar to the uploaded eBook.
- **Price suggestion**: The AI's recommended price, accompanied by its reasoning based on similar products and store context.
- **Category suggestions**: Recommended categories for the new product, mapped to your existing store categories.

## Step 3: Edit and Create Draft

Below the analysis results, you will find the **Review and create product** form. This form is pre-populated with the AI's generated draft content.

1. Review and edit the **Title**, **Short description**, and **Long description**.
2. Adjust the **Regular price** if needed.
3. Select or deselect **Categories** (the AI's suggestions are pre-checked).
4. Review the comma-separated **Tags**. Only tags that already exist in your store will be applied.
5. **Downloadable Options**:
   - Check **Attach PDF as downloadable file** to automatically attach the uploaded PDF to the WooCommerce product.
   - Check **Make product downloadable** to mark the product as downloadable in WooCommerce.
6. Click **Create WooCommerce Draft**.

<Callout kind="warning">
If your server does not support the required image processing libraries (Imagick or Ghostscript), the plugin will display a notice that cover and gallery images cannot be generated. You will need to add product images manually in WooCommerce after creating the draft.
</Callout>

## Step 4: Finalize in WooCommerce

After the draft is created, a success message will appear with a link to **Edit product**. Click this link to open the standard WooCommerce product editor, where you can:

- Review the final layout.
- Upload or adjust product images (if not generated automatically).
- Configure inventory, variations, or advanced product settings.
- Publish the product to your live store.
