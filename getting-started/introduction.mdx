## What eBook Product Assistant is

eBook Product Assistant turns uploaded PDF eBooks into WooCommerce draft products using AI. You upload a PDF, the plugin analyzes it, compares the result against your existing catalog, and prepares a draft product for you to review before you publish anything.

## What It Does

The plugin extracts text from each PDF on your server and sends a truncated portion to your configured AI provider for analysis. You can use OpenAI, Claude, or OpenRouter to help identify product details from the uploaded eBook.

After analysis, the plugin compares the result against your existing WooCommerce catalog. It then prepares product copy, pricing suggestions, and category recommendations based on what it found in the PDF and how that information fits with the products you already have.

Nothing is published automatically. Every product the plugin creates is saved as a **Draft**, so you can review the title, description, pricing, categories, and any other details before it goes live.

## Where to Find It

When WooCommerce is active, open **WooCommerce → eBook Assistant** to use the plugin. If WooCommerce is inactive, the plugin appears under **Tools → eBook Assistant** instead.

Inside the plugin screen, you work from tabs for **Single PDF** and **Batch** processing. Use **Single PDF** when you want to review one upload at a time, or **Batch** when you want to process multiple PDFs in one session.
